Our Story

PizzaDelight was founded in 2005 by the Rossi family, who brought their authentic Italian recipes from Naples to create a pizza experience like no other. What started as a small family-owned pizzeria has grown into a beloved local chain, but we've never lost sight of our roots.

Our commitment to quality ingredients, traditional techniques, and exceptional customer service has been the cornerstone of our success. Every pizza is handcrafted with love and attention to detail, just as it was on day one.

Today, PizzaDelight continues to be family-owned and operated, with the second generation of Rossis now leading our expansion while maintaining the authentic flavors and warm hospitality that made us a neighborhood favorite.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at PizzaDelight.

Quality Ingredients

We source only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients for our pizzas. From locally grown vegetables to premium imported cheeses, we never compromise on quality.

Handcrafted Excellence

Every pizza is handcrafted by our skilled pizza artisans. We believe in the traditional methods that bring out the best flavors and textures in our food.

Community First

We're proud to be part of the community we serve. We support local initiatives, source locally when possible, and strive to make a positive impact in our neighborhood.
